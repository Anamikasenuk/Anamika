Anamika Sengupta- A Snapshot


About Me

Hi, I’m Anamika Sengupta, a web developer with a background in textile and fashion technology. After spending more than two decades immersed in the world of textiles, design, fashion and technology, I decided to pursue my passion for coding and web development. Today, I blend creativity with technical expertise to create intuitive, user-centered websites and applications.

My Journey: From Textile/Fashion Technology to Web Development:

I am a Gold Medalist in B. Tech in textile and fashion technology from the University of Calcutta and a certified Sustainable Fashion professional from London College of Fashion. I worked on the cutting edge of design, production, and technology. Whether it was developing new fabrics, improving production processes, or creating innovative solutions for the fashion industry, I was constantly solving problems and thinking creatively. This experience laid a strong foundation for my transition into web development, where my technical knowledge and eye for design have become key assets.

As I delved into the world of coding, I realized that many of the skills I had developed—attention to detail, problem-solving, and a deep understanding of how technology can shape user experiences—were directly transferable to web development. I quickly picked up HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React, and I now focus on creating websites that are as functional as they are visually appealing.

What I Bring to the Table

Coming from the intersection of fashion and technology, I offer a unique perspective as a web developer. My background equips me with: • Design and User Experience: In fashion technology, understanding aesthetics and user preferences is key. I bring that same understanding to web design, ensuring that websites not only look great but also provide a seamless user experience. • Technical Precision: Working in fashion technology required precision in every aspect, from material specifications to production efficiency. I apply the same meticulous approach to writing clean, efficient code. • Creative Problem-Solving: Whether it was innovating with new materials or designing efficient workflows, creativity was a core part of my work. I bring that creativity to web development, finding innovative solutions for complex challenges. • Industry Adaptability: My experience in an ever-evolving industry has taught me to adapt quickly to new trends and technologies, making me a fast learner in the dynamic world of web development.

My Skills

I specialize in:

• Front-End Development: Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build responsive, visually appealing websites. • Back-End Development: Creating robust server-side applications with Node.js and Express, along with database management (MongoDB, MySQL). • UX/UI Design: Drawing on my fashion background to craft user-friendly interfaces that balance form and function. • Creative Solutions: Applying out-of-the-box thinking to design and code, ensuring that every project is both practical and unique.

Why Work with Me?

My transition from textile and fashion technology to web development allows me to bring a fresh, creative approach to building websites. I understand the importance of both aesthetics and functionality, and I have a proven track record of working in fast-paced, dynamic industries. Whether you’re looking for a sleek, modern website or a complex web application, I’m ready to bring your ideas to life.

Let’s Collaborate

I’m always excited to work with new clients and tackle exciting challenges in the world of web development. Let’s combine creativity and technology to create something exceptional!
